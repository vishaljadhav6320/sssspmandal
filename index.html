Mission

Mental retardation among persons with disabilities is a serious problem in society In many forms of disability, mental retardation is such that other divyangs can overcome the power of intelligence by standing on their own feet. A mentally retarded person, however, is a very serious problem in the family. It is a bad subject in society, due to the lack of gift of intelligence to the mentally retarded person, he has to be taken care of by someone till the end, due to which many problems arise in the family, especially after the parents, the big question is who should take care of them. In order to solve the problems of the mentally retarded person and to rehabilitate the mentally retarded person, efforts are being made to inculcate values through rehabilitation of mentally retarded persons in Gojubavi, Taluka - Baramati District-Pune. Rehabilitative work is carried out in music therapy, occupational therapy, conversation therapy, etc. in the rehabilitation center for the mentally retarded persons.
